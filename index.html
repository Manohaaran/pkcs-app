🔑 Set Up Smart Scan
The Smart Scan feature reads your handwritten pages and fills the fields automatically. It needs a one-time API key to work. Your key is stored only on this device and is never shared.
Enter your key above
Get your key at console.anthropic.com → API Keys → Create Key. The key starts with sk-ant-
Privacy: Your key is stored only in this browser on this device. It is used solely to power the Smart Scan. If you clear your browser data, you will need to enter it again. Do not share this key with anyone.
No key? You can skip this and fill the fields manually after photographing your page. The rest of the app works fully without a key.
